From the Central Valley of California. He is a interdisciplanry artist living during 2005-now that relieves his stresses of daily life onto his canvas and medium of choice in order to cope to the modern life he hopes to rebuild through his art. Nevertheless his unique style embodies the main principles of such as line, shape, color, value, texture, and reduces all of the elements to its very basic form(s) to represent a story or figures. Thus he has a varied amount of topics that he expierences first hand and second hand from his obersavtion.
